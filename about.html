About us

Nestled in the heart of the city center, Zestia offers an unparalleled dining experience on Cvjetni trg. Our prime location immerses you in the vibrant energy of the bustling streets, while providing a serene oasis where you can escape the hustle and bustle of urban life. Whether you're exploring the city's cultural attractions or seeking a moment of culinary indulgence, Zestia's central location makes it the perfect destination for locals and visitors alike. Join us and discover a hidden gem in the heart of Zagreb, where every meal is a celebration of flavor and elegance.

Step into Zestia's sleek and modern space, where contemporary design meets culinary excellence. Our restaurant exudes an inviting ambiance with clean lines, stylish furnishings, and subtle touches of sophistication. From the chic decor to the innovative lighting, every element of our space is thoughtfully curated to provide a comfortable yet upscale dining experience.

Whether you're seated at a cozy booth, gathered around our sleek bar, or enjoying the fresh air on our outdoor terrace, you'll find yourself immersed in an atmosphere of refined comfort and hospitality. Join us at Zestia and experience the perfect blend of modern sophistication and timeless charm in every moment spent with us.
